06 · Contributions & Limitations
23 / 25

Contributions

Reframe
The teacher-mother is an institutionally bordered position rather than a private role conflict.
Name
Affective bordering names how patience, praise, and self-restraint are pulled from women teachers as unpaid work.
Compare
A situated Chinese case enters dialogue with Korean and Indonesian feminist scholarship.Park (2007); Adriany (2018)
Redistribute
Responsibility must be redrawn across school, family, and society.

Limits & Future

Sample
Only three teachers in Jiangsu cannot represent regional and structural variation.
Method
The family field relies on self-report, not in-home observation.
Scope
Sino-Korean and Sino-Japanese comparison has not yet been developed.
Next
Affective bordering can extend to other feminised care professions.nursing · ECE · social work
